Brief Introduction

SAT

The SAT also known as the Scholastic Aptitude Test is taken
by millions of high school students across the world in order
to attend college in the U.S. This test is comprised of three sections, including
Math, Critical Reading, Grammar, and an optional Essay Section.
It is usually taken by 11th and 12th
graders and is used as a standard in most colleges.
Thus, scoring high is essential on this test. Understanding that aspect, students
go around looking for profitable places.

ACT

The ACT also known as the American College Testing is also taken by millions
of high school students around the world in order to attend college in the U.S.
The exam consists of Math, Science, Critical Reading, Grammar, and an
optional essay section. This test is known for being fast paced and makes sure the
high school students are college-ready.
