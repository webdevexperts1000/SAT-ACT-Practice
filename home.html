The SAT/ACT Practice




Brief Introduction

The SAT also known as the Scholastic Aptitude Test is taken by millions of high school students across the world in order to attend college in the U.S.
This test is comprised of three sections, including Math, Critical Reading, Grammar, and an optional Essay Section. It is usually taken by 11th and 12th
graders and is used as a standard in most colleges. Thus, scoring high is essential on this test. Understanding that aspect, students go around looking for profitable places.
